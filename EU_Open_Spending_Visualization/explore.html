What am I seeing?

To the left you can find several views on data concerning grants and commitments by the European Commision from the years 2007 till 2013

By default you will see a map showcasing, by means of colorcoding, the total amount of money each country received. Beneath the map is a bar chart showing how much each category of EU department has spended. To the right of the bar chart is a line graph, initially showing the total amount of money from 2007 till 2013. Lastly there is a table showing the total amount each country has received.

How to use this?

Several interactions are possible:

  • Zoom: if you want to zoom in on regions with small countries, use your scrollwheel.

  • Moving the map: drag the map to show other parts of the world.

  • Show data per year: if you move your mouse over the map or bar chart the line chart will adjust accordingly.

  • Inspect the data yourself: use the download button beneath to download the cleaned and where needed consolidated data files. These are in machine readable (human understandable) (JSON) format.
